Overview

The Gosaikunda Trek is a short yet stunning Himalayan journey that begins with a scenic 45-minute drive from Kathmandu to Sundarijal or Dhunche, depending on your route. Ideal for trekkers seeking a mix of natural beauty and cultural exploration, this trail leads to the sacred alpine lakes of Gosaikunda, nestled at an altitude of around 4,380 meters.

You'll trek through lush rhododendron forests, bamboo groves, and high alpine terrain inside Langtang National Park, encountering Tamang and Sherpa communities along the way. A unique highlight is visiting the traditional yak cheese factory in Sing Gompa, offering insight into local life and a taste of fresh Himalayan cheese.

This moderate trek offers breathtaking views of Langtang Lirung, Ganesh Himal, and Manaslu, with peaceful trails far from the crowds of Everest or Annapurna. The sacred lakes of Gosaikunda, revered by both Hindus and Buddhists, provide a spiritually enriching and scenic destination surrounded by rugged snow-capped peaks.

Suitable for both experienced hikers and fit beginners, the Gosaikunda Trek takes around 6 to 8 days and reaches its highest point at Lauribina Pass (4,610m). With a balance of nature, culture, and altitude, it’s a perfect choice for those seeking a rewarding trekking experience close to Kathmandu.

Itinerary

Day 1: Drive to Sundarijal and Trek to Chisopani (2,150m / 7,054ft)

Drive Duration: 45 minutes | Trek Duration: 4–5 hours

Start with a short drive from Kathmandu to Sundarijal. Enter Shivapuri National Park and begin your ascent through lush forests, passing small Tamang settlements before reaching Chisopani.

Overnight: Teahouse in Chisopani

Day 2: Trek to Kutumsang (2,470m / 8,103ft)

Trek Duration: 6–7 hours

Descend to Pati Bhanjyang, then continue uphill through meadows and rhododendron forests to reach Kutumsang. Enjoy distant views of Langtang and Ganesh Himal.

Overnight: Teahouse in Kutumsang

Day 3: Trek to Ghopte (3,400m / 11,152ft)

Trek Duration: 6–7 hours

Leave Kutumsang and begin a steady uphill climb through dense rhododendron and fir forests. Enjoy views of snow-capped peaks as you pass through Magen Goth and cross forested ridges. Continue on a quieter trail toward Ghopte, a peaceful stop nestled beneath the Laurebina Pass.

Overnight: Teahouse in Ghopte

Day 4: Trek to Gosaikunda (4,380m / 14,370ft)

Trek Duration: 6–7 hours

Follow a dramatic ridgeline trail with amazing Himalayan views as you climb to Lauribina. Continue to the sacred Gosaikunda Lakes, a serene and spiritually significant alpine destination.

Overnight: Teahouse near Gosaikunda Lake

Day 5: Trek to Sing Gompa (3,580m / 11,745ft)

Trek Duration: 5–6 hours

Descend from Gosaikunda along a forested trail to Sing Gompa. Visit the local cheese factory and enjoy peaceful surroundings in this monastery village nestled in the forest.

Overnight: Teahouse in Sing Gompa

Day 6: Trek to Dhunche and Drive to Kathmandu

Trek Duration: 3 hours | Drive Duration: 6–7 hours

Enjoy a relaxed morning hike downhill from Sing Gompa through pine and oak forests to reach Dhunche. After lunch, take a scenic drive back to Kathmandu, passing through picturesque villages and riverside landscapes.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

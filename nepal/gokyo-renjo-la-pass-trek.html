Gokyo Renjo La Pass Trek Overview

The Gokyo Renjo La Pass Trek is one of the most scenic and rewarding trekking routes in Nepal’s Everest region. Ideal for adventurers seeking a quieter, off-the-beaten-path alternative to the classic Everest Base Camp trail, this trek combines high-altitude adventure, glacial beauty, and rich Sherpa culture.

At the heart of the journey lies the spectacular Gokyo Valley, home to the sacred, glacier-fed Gokyo Lakes—some of the highest freshwater lakes in the world. These turquoise gems are surrounded by towering Himalayan peaks, including Everest, Lhotse, Cho Oyu, and Makalu.

A sunrise climb to Gokyo Ri (5,357m) offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Himalayan giants and the vast Ngozumpa Glacier, the longest glacier in Nepal. The thrill continues as you cross the stunning Renjo La Pass (5,360m), one of the region’s most beautiful high passes, offering a dramatic descent into remote alpine terrain.

Throughout the trek, you’ll experience authentic Sherpa hospitality, visit traditional villages, and explore ancient monasteries steeped in Buddhist tradition. Comfortable teahouses along the route provide hearty meals, warm shelter, and a welcoming glimpse into mountain life.

The trek begins and ends with a scenic flight from Kathmandu to Lukla. It’s best suited for trekkers with moderate fitness levels who are prepared for the challenges of altitude and long trekking days. With proper acclimatization, this journey is both safe and incredibly fulfilling.

The Gokyo and Renjo La Trek is a hidden gem—perfect for nature lovers, photography enthusiasts, and trekkers looking to avoid the crowds while immersing themselves in Nepal’s raw Himalayan wilderness.

Gokyo Renjo La Pass Trek Highlights

  • Trek to the serene Gokyo Lakes—glacial, turquoise waters among the highest freshwater lakes on Earth
  • Climb Gokyo Ri (5,357m) for unforgettable sunrise views over Everest, Cho Oyu, Lhotse, and Makalu
  • Cross Renjo La Pass (5,360m), one of the most scenic high passes in the Everest region
  • Admire panoramic views of the Ngozumpa Glacier—the longest glacier in Nepal—right from the teahouses and trails around Gokyo
  • Immerse in authentic Sherpa culture by visiting remote villages like Dole, Machhermo, and Thame
  • Visit ancient monasteries and learn about Tibetan Buddhist traditions in the Khumbu region
  • Enjoy peaceful trails and alpine landscapes away from the busy Everest Base Camp route
  • Explore Namche Bazaar, a vibrant Sherpa hub full of culture, cafes, and mountain views
  • Walk across the iconic Hillary Suspension Bridge over the rushing Dudh Koshi River
  • Trek through Sagarmatha National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site rich in flora and fauna

Gokyo Renjo La Pass Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,350m / 4,428ft)

Arrive in Kathmandu, the vibrant capital of Nepal. After your arrival, you'll be transferred to your hotel to relax. In the evening, attend a trek briefing to prepare for your Gokyo Trek adventure.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Day 2: Flight to Lukla (2,860m / 9,383ft) & Trek to Phakding (2,652m / 8,698ft)

Drive Time: 4 hours and 30 minutes (Kathmandu to Ramechhap)

Flight Time: 15 minutes from Ramechhap | Trek Duration: 3–4 hours

Take a scenic flight to Lukla, the gateway to Everest. Your trek begins as you descend through Chaurikharka Village, following the Dudh Koshi River to the charming village of Phakding.

Overnight: Teahouse in Phakding

Day 3: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m / 11,286ft)

Trek Duration: 6–7 hours

Trek through lush forests and cross iconic suspension bridges, including the Hillary Bridge. Enter Sagarmatha National Park and arrive at Namche Bazaar, a vibrant Sherpa town and key acclimatization stop.

Overnight: Teahouse in Namche Bazaar

Day 4: Acclimatization Day in Namche Bazaar

This day is dedicated to acclimatization. Take a short hike to Everest View Hotel (3,880m) for breathtaking views of Everest, Ama Dablam, and more. Visit the Sherpa Culture Museum or explore the local market.

Overnight: Teahouse in Namche Bazaar

Day 5: Trek to Dole (4,110m / 13,484ft)

Trek Duration: 6 hours

Walk through rhododendron forests with sweeping views of Everest and Ama Dablam. Visit Tengboche Monastery, a spiritual hub with incredible views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks.

Overnight: Teahouse in Dole

Day 6: Trek to Machhermo (4,410m / 14,468ft)

Trek Duration: 5–6 hours

Cross the Imja Khola river and pass through Pangboche. Arrive at Machhermo, an important stop before reaching the higher elevations of the Gokyo region.

Overnight: Teahouse in Machhermo

Day 7: Trek to Gokyo (4,910m / 16,109ft)

Trek Duration: 5–6 hours

Hike past the Thukla Pass memorials dedicated to fallen climbers and continue to Gokyo, a stunning village set beside the famous Gokyo Lakes.

Overnight: Teahouse in Gokyo

Day 8: Acclimatization Day in Gokyo

This day is reserved for acclimatization. Hike to the top of Gokyo Ri (5,357m) for spectacular views of Mount Everest, Cho Oyu, Lhotse, and the surrounding peaks.

Overnight: Teahouse in Gokyo

Day 9: Cross Renjo La Pass (5,360m / 17,585ft) & Trek to Lungden (4,210m / 13,810ft)

Trek Duration: 7–8 hours

Climb the final high pass of the trek, Renjo La, with breathtaking Himalayan panoramas. Descend through remote landscapes and yak pastures to Lungden.

Overnight: Teahouse in Lungden

Day 10: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m / 11,283ft)

Trek Duration: 7-8 hours

Descend along a scenic trail passing through the peaceful village of Thame. Rejoin the main Everest route and return to Namche for a warm shower and hearty meal.

Overnight: Teahouse in Namche Bazaar

Day 11: Trek to Lukla (2,860m / 9,383ft)

Trek Duration: 6–7 hours

Retrace your steps back to Lukla, passing Tengboche and other villages along the way. Celebrate your journey as you return to Lukla for your flight back to Kathmandu.

Overnight: Teahouse in Lukla

Day 12: Flight to Kathmandu

Flight Time: 35 minutes

Fly back to Kathmandu and enjoy the rest of your day relaxing or exploring Kathmandu’s iconic sights, such as Swayambhunath, Boudhanath, or Pashupatinath Temple.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Day 13: Departure from Kathmandu

Transfer to Tribhuvan International Airport for your flight home. Your unforgettable Gokyo Trek comes to an end, but the memories will last a lifetime.

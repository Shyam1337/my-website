Overview

The Chisopani Nagarkot Trek is a perfect short trekking option near Kathmandu, combining stunning Himalayan views, peaceful nature trails, and rich local culture. Ideal for those with limited time in Nepal or looking for a gentle introduction to trekking, this route offers an easy yet rewarding adventure without the need for long travel or domestic flights.

This trek takes you along the scenic ridges of the Kathmandu Valley, through Shivapuri National Park and charming hill villages like Chisopani, offering panoramic sunrise and sunset views over the Langtang, Dorje Lakpa, Gaurishankar, and even Everest ranges on clear days. Youâ€™ll pass through lush forests, terraced fields, and rural settlements, experiencing authentic Nepali life away from the bustle of the city.

With a maximum altitude of just over 2,100 meters (6,890 feet), the Chisopani Nagarkot Trek is suitable for families, beginner trekkers, and anyone seeking a low-altitude Himalayan experience. The trek can be completed in 2 to 3 days, making it one of the most accessible and time-efficient treks in Nepal.

Whether you're after breathtaking mountain scenery, a peaceful nature escape, or a cultural walk through traditional villages, the Chisopani to Nagarkot Trek is a compact but beautiful journey just a short drive from Kathmandu.

Itinerary

Day 1: Drive to Sundarijal & Trek to Chisopani (2,150m / 7,054ft)

Drive Duration: 1 hour | Trek Duration: 4â€“5 hours

Begin the trek with a short scenic drive from Kathmandu to Sundarijal. Enter Shivapuri National Park and trek through dense forests, waterfalls, and traditional Tamang villages. Reach Chisopani by afternoon for stunning mountain views and a peaceful village atmosphere.

Overnight: Teahouse in Chisopani

Day 2: Trek from Chisopani to Nagarkot (2,175m / 7,136ft)

Trek Duration: 6â€“7 hours

Enjoy sunrise views of the Langtang range before starting your trek. The trail continues along scenic ridgelines, pine forests, and rural settlements with panoramic views of the Himalayas. Arrive in Nagarkot, famous for its breathtaking sunsets and Himalayan vistas.

Overnight: Hotel or guesthouse in Nagarkot

Trek Duration: 3â€“4 hours | Drive Duration: 1.5 hours

After sunrise and breakfast in Nagarkot, trek downhill through small villages and forest trails to the UNESCO-listed Changunarayan Temple. Explore the historic site before driving back to Kathmandu, marking the end of your short yet rewarding trek.

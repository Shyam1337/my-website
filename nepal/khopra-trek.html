Khopra Trek Overview

The Khopra Trek is an undiscovered treasure in the Annapurna region of Nepal. This Khopra trail offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and serves as a peaceful alternative to more crowded trekking routes. Our Khopra trek itinerary provides all the details you need, from permits to the best trekking seasons in Nepal, making it suitable for both novice and experienced trekkers.

The Khopra Trekking, also called the Khopra Ridge Trek, is a hidden trekking trail in Nepal that combines natural beauty, cultural experiences, and adventures off the beaten path. Khopra Danda trekking sits at an altitude of 3,660 meters, offering spectacular Himalayan panoramas and a tranquil trekking experience away from the busier Annapurna Circuit.

During the trek, hikers enjoy stunning views of prominent Himalayan peaks such as Dhaulagiri, Annapurna South, Annapurna I, and Nilgiri. The trail passes through Gurung villages like Ghandruk and Tadapani, providing authentic cultural interactions along with memorable Khopra trek views of Machhapuchhre (Fishtail) and Hiunchuli.

This trek is known for its varied terrain, including lush rhododendron forests, terraced fields, alpine ridges, and isolated mountain paths. Trekkers can experience the warmth and hospitality of the Annapurna region by staying in community-operated lodges and traditional teahouses, making the journey both culturally enriching and adventurous.

A highlight of Khopra trek is the optional excursion to Khayar Lake, an alpine site at 4,500 meters elevation. Surrounded by Fang Peak, this sacred location provides a moment of peace amidst the Himalayas, and the Khayar Lake Trek is considered one of the most rewarding parts of the route.

Whether you are an experienced hiker or a beginner seeking a moderately challenging journey, the Khopra Trekking offers a perfect combination of natural beauty, cultural richness, and memorable mountain vistas. With its well-planned Khopra hike itinerary and the stunning views of Khopra trek, it remains one of Nepalâ€™s most remarkable hidden trekking trails.

Khopra Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m / 4,592ft)

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Welcome to Nepal! Our team will meet you at the airport and transfer you to your hotel in Kathmandu. Rest and prepare for the adventure ahead.

Day 2: Fly to Pokhara & Trek to Ghandruk (1,900m / 6,232ft)

Flight Time: 25 minutes

Drive Time: 2 hours

Trek Time: 3 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Ghandruk

Enjoy a scenic flight to Pokhara with views of Dhaulagiri, Manaslu, and Annapurna. After a short drive to Syauli Bazaar, begin trekking to the picturesque Gurung village of Ghandruk, rich in culture and tradition.

Day 3: Trek from Ghandruk to Tadapani (2,590m / 8,497ft)

Trek Time: 3â€“4 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Tadapani

Ascend through lush rhododendron and magnolia forests with possible sightings of Nepal gray langurs. Tadapani offers stunning views of Annapurna South, Hiunchuli, and Machhapuchhre.

Day 4: Trek from Tadapani to Dobato (3,350m / 10,990ft)

Trek Time: 6 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Dobato

Leave the main trail for a secluded path through dense forest. The route offers quiet surroundings and panoramic views of Dhaulagiri and Annapurna South.

Day 5: Trek from Dobato to Chistibung (2,975m / 9,760ft)

Trek Time: 5â€“6 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Chistibung

Descend and ascend through thick woodlands to reach Chistibung, a seasonal settlement. Keep an eye out for Himalayan Monals and Tahr along the way.

Day 6: Trek from Chistibung to Khopra Danda (3,660m / 12,007ft)

Trek Time: 3â€“4 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Khopra Danda

Ascend above the tree line to reach Khopra Ridge, where breathtaking views of Annapurna South, Dhaulagiri, and Nilgiri await. A nearby viewpoint overlooks the Kali Gandaki gorge.

Day 7: Day Hike to Khayar Lake (4,500m / 14,763ft)

Trek Time: 7â€“8 hours (optional)

Overnight: Teahouse in Khopra Danda

Take an early morning hike to sacred Khayar Lake, surrounded by alpine scenery and Fang Peak. Alternatively, enjoy a shorter hike and rest at the ridge.

Day 8: Trek from Khopra Danda to Swanta Village (2,200m / 7,217ft)

Trek Time: 6 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Swanta Village

Descend steeply to Swanta, a quiet Magar village surrounded by terraced fields and mountain views, especially of Dhaulagiri.

Day 9: Trek from Swanta Village to Ulleri (2,050m / 6,725ft) via Ghorepani

Trek Time: 6 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Ulleri

Pass through Chittre and rejoin the Annapurna trail in Ghorepani. Descend through rhododendron forests to reach Ulleri.

Day 10: Trek from Ulleri to Nayapul, Drive to Pokhara (822m / 2,696ft)

Trek Time: 4 hours

Drive Time: 2 hours

Overnight: Hotel in Pokhara

Enjoy the final trekking day as you hike down to Nayapul, then drive back to Pokhara where you can relax and reflect on your journey.

Day 11: Sightseeing in Pokhara & Fly to Kathmandu

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Visit the Peace Stupa for a sunrise view, explore Pokhara's lakeside, then catch a short afternoon flight to Kathmandu. Enjoy a farewell dinner in the evening.

Day 12: Departure from Kathmandu

After breakfast, our team will transfer you to the airport for your flight home. Thank you for trekking with us!

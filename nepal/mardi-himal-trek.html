Mardi Himal Trek Overview

The Mardi Himal Trek is a rewarding and scenic short trek in Nepal, offering a perfect blend of solitude, stunning mountain views, and genuine cultural immersion. Often called a hidden gem trek in Nepal, it serves as an excellent alternative to the more crowded Annapurna Base Camp (ABC) trek for those seeking a peaceful Himalayan experience. This Mardi Himal trek is accessible to both beginners and moderately experienced trekkers, making it an ideal choice for a short yet fulfilling Himalayan trekking adventure.

The Mardi Himal is located in the heart of the Annapurna region. You'll trek along quiet paths through lush rhododendron and oak forests, through traditional Gurung villages, and up to dramatic ridgelines with panoramic Himalayan vistas.

The Mardi Himal trek begins after a short drive from Pokhara to Phedi or Kande by bus, taxi or jeep. As you gradually ascend into the high alpine terrain, you'll be rewarded with breathtaking views of: Machapuchare (Fishtail, 6,993m), Annapurna South (7,219m) and Hiunchuli (6,441m)

The highlight of this short Annapurna-style trek is reaching the Mardi Himal viewpoint and High Camp (3,580m). Here, trekkers feel an incredible proximity to the towering snow-capped peaks. This section is among the most rewarding Pokhara trekking routes for adventure seekers looking for an accessible high-altitude experience.

Unlike busier Nepal treks, the Mardi Himal offers a deeper connection with local communities. You'll encounter fewer trekkers, allowing for more authentic interactions with the local people in teahouses and homestays along the way. Here, you can experience Gurung culture firsthand, enjoy warm hospitality, and taste authentic Nepali cuisine, which enriches the entire journey.

Whether you are a beginner looking for a manageable high-altitude trek or an experienced hiker searching for quieter trails in the Himalayas, the Mardi Himal Trek delivers an unforgettable experience. It offers a unique blend of stunning scenery, cultural immersion, and peaceful natural beauty, making it a favorite for those who prioritize a more intimate trekking adventure.

Mardi Himal Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu – Welcome to Nepal

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Arrive in Kathmandu, the bustling capital of Nepal. Our team will greet you at the airport and transfer you to your hotel. Relax and prepare for your upcoming Mardi Himal trekking adventure through the Himalayas.

Day 2: Fly to Pokhara, Drive to Phedi & Trek to Pothana (1,925m / 6,314ft)

Flight Time: 25 minutes

Drive Time: 30 minutes

Trek Time: 4 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Pothana

Take a scenic flight to Pokhara, followed by a short drive to Phedi, the trailhead. Begin your trek through lush forests and terraced fields to reach the peaceful village of Pothana with beautiful mountain views.

Day 3: Trek from Pothana to Forest Camp (2,600m / 8,528ft)

Trek Time: 5 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Forest Camp

Walk through enchanting forests filled with rhododendron, maple, and oak trees. In spring, the trails come alive with vibrant blooms. Enjoy a simple yet cozy teahouse stay in Forest Camp.

Day 4: Trek from Forest Camp to Low Camp (3,150m / 10,332ft)

Trek Time: 5 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Low Camp

Continue ascending through thinning forest. As elevation increases, stunning views of Machhapuchhre (Fishtail Peak) emerge. Enjoy a warm meal beside an open fire in the teahouse dining hall.

Day 5: Trek from Low Camp to High Camp (3,600m / 11,811ft)

Trek Time: 4 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in High Camp

Today you’ll leave the forest behind and hike along a ridgeline with panoramic views of Annapurna South and Hiunchuli. High Camp offers a scenic spot with basic but comfortable lodging.

Day 6: Hike to Mardi Himal Viewpoint & Badal Danda (4,200m / 13,776ft, 3200m/10496ft)

Trek Time: 6-7 hours (round trip)

Overnight: Teahouse in Badal Danda

Today is the most scenic day of the Mardi Himal Trek. Start early and hike to the Upper Viewpoint at 4,200 meters for breathtaking sunrise views of Annapurna I, Machhapuchhre (Fishtail Peak), and Mardi Himal. After soaking in the panoramic Himalayan vistas, return to the teahouse at High Camp for a hearty breakfast. Later, continue hiking down to Badal Danda, a peaceful ridge known for its cloud-covered valleys and sweeping mountain views. This unforgettable day offers the most iconic moments of the trek.

Day 7: Trek Down to Bhaisikharka/Siding Village (1,750m / 5,740ft)

Trek Time: 7 hours

Overnight: Teahouse in Siding

Descend through a different trail to Siding, a traditional village untouched by mainstream trekking. Experience warm hospitality and enjoy a home-cooked Nepali meal with a local family.

Day 8: Trek to Lumre & Drive to Pokhara

Trek Time: 3 hours

Drive Time: 1.5 hours

Overnight: Hotel in Pokhara

Enjoy a gentle walk through terraced hills and small settlements to reach Lumre. A private vehicle will transfer you back to Pokhara, where you can relax by the lakeside in the evening.

Day 9: Drive from Pokhara to Kathmandu

Drive Time: 6-7 hours

Optional Flight: 25 minutes (US$125)

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Travel back to Kathmandu via a scenic highway route. Upon arrival, enjoy a restful evening or explore local markets before your farewell dinner at a traditional Nepali restaurant.

Day 10: Departure from Kathmandu

Activity: Airport transfer

After breakfast, you’ll be transferred to Tribhuvan International Airport for your onward journey. Farewell from Nepal—carry unforgettable memories of your Mardi Himal Trek!

  • Daily hot drinks – tea, coffee, or hot chocolate (3 times per day during the trek)

About Us

Meet the Founder

Above & Beyond Trekking Company is owned and operated by Shyam (Sam) Bahadur Pun, a local trekking guide who grew up in the foothills of the Annapurnas. With over 20 years of trekking experience in the Himalayas, Sam has travelled extensively throughout the Nepalese mountains and loves sharing these experiences with guests.

Legitimacy & Memberships

The company is officially registered under "Wonders for Nepal" (Registration Number 51327), and is a proud member of:

  • Trekking Agencies Association of Nepal (TAAN)
  • Nepal Mountaineering Association (NMA)
  • Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation

Our Team

Our guides are selected for their expertise and deep local knowledge — many of whom grew up in villages within the region. They are committed to making your journey safe, comfortable, and unforgettable. All guides are trained in First Environmental standards to help preserve our mountains for future generations.

We Look Forward to Hearing From You!

Whether you're planning your first trek or your tenth, we’re excited to help you explore the incredible Himalayas. Reach out with your questions anytime — we’re here to guide you every step of the way.
